TOP 8 Night Time Healthy Snacks - Eat This NOT That!

  • Hot Chocolate

    • Instead of this go for a decaffeinated coffee. It is gonna have 50 calories at most and you can add something for cream or chocolate sauce.

  • Cereal

    • Cereal is bad, it has so many calories and sugar and you can eat a whole box. Instead of regular cereals, go for puff cereals. They are low dense in calories reported to it's volume.

  • Yogurt

    • Go for greek yogurt instead. It has around 60 calorie and 8gr of protein per 100gr. Be sure to choose the ones with 0% fat.

  • Pie

    • You don't even put it in a plate, you eat half a pie and it is not even gonna do anything for you, it has many calories and sugar.
    Take an apple, cut it into slices, put some artificial sweetener, microwave for a minute and put it into that greek yogurt if you want to.

  • Chocolate bars

    • You can try protein bars instead and you can make them at home if you want to

  • Ice Cream

    • Who uses a bowl ? You eat the whole box and you got yourself with 2000-3000 calories surplus. Try instead greek yogurt ice cream bars.
    Even if you eat like 5 of them, you got 500 calories. It is better than 2000!

  • Peanut butter

    • Do not even have it in the house ! Go for Powdered Peanut Butter (PB2). It has 70% lower fat content and you can consume your dose without extra calories.

  • Salty snacks

    • The number one diet killer. Choose popcorn ! Has as much popcorn you want and it is still better than one bag of chips.
